![Band Together](banner.png)

A story mod that was started during
the [2024 Single Planet Mod Jam](https://outerwildsmods.com/jam/mar-2024/) and
was finished afterwards. Embark on a quest for community on the planet Fractured
Harmony, and reunite that which was broken long ago.

## Frequently Asked Questions (Spoilers)
<details>
<summary>Why can't I talk to this NPC anymore?</summary>
<br>
If this happened after you ejected your cockpit, re-enter the ship through the hole made by the cockpit, then exit again through the hatch. This bug is caused by one of the other Jam 3 mods, so it can also be fixed by disabling the other mods.
</details>
